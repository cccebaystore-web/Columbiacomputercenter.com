About Us

Proud Ownership

David Hutchinson has been the proud owner of Columbia Computer Center since the opening on March 2nd, 2001. With over 20 years of running the business, he has always strived to provide customers with the best service possible.

Repair Services

We strive to provide the best repair services to all of our customers. At fair rates and with knowledge and understanding, we provide top of the line repair services.

Refurbished Desktops and Laptops

Refurbished computers at Columbia Computer Center don't skimp on quality. With Solid State Drives and meaningful precise installation of Windows 10 or 11, all of our devices are ready to go the second you leave the door.

Custom PCs: Satisfaction Guaranteed

Come in and order a Custom PC that fits your budget. Regardless of if it's gaming at the highest frames at home or editing and planning without any delay in the office, we can work with your budget and build a custom PC for you.

We do E-Recycling!

Recycle old or unwanted electronics, cords, or equipment! Just come in the doors and our staff will be happy to help you recycle your electronics.
