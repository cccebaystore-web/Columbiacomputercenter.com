Frequently Asked Questions

Please reach us at ccc@cccwireless.net if you cannot find an answer to your question.
Do you guys sell refurbished or new computers?
Selling refurbished laptops and desktops allows us to properly test and put our input as repairmen on what the best parts are for you. We warranty our products for a year and give a two year warranty on the hard drives inside. However, we can order in new laptops or desktops in for you if you specifically request.
Can I recycle my TV/CRT with you?
No. Due to size restraints of new televisions and government restrictions on CRT's, we cannot. However you are free to bring anything else electronic. Printers, wires, appliances, etc.
Can I pay over the phone?
It is possible to purchase something over the phone, however to get an accurate display of our products and availability it is best to come in.
I'm hacked, what do I do?
Come in and get checked in for a tune up! Tune ups are 100 dollars and rid you completely of any malware, spyware, adware or remote access software.
My laptop/desktop is running slow
You might be eligible for a Solid State Drive Upgrade. Talk to a technician and have them check and see if you are on a slower, less reliable hard drive. If you are, we can get you on a Solid State Drive! The price can vary depending on storage. For an accurate quote, give us a call or come in.
Do you fix Macbooks/Imac's?
At the moment, we only support Windows Laptops and Desktops.
